Machine Operating Manual

How the Machine Works:

This machine operates in three distinct steps to ensure efficient processing:

Step 1: Power On
Press and hold the power button for 3 seconds until the LED indicator turns green. Wait for the initialization sequence to complete (approximately 10 seconds).

Step 2: Load Material
Open the loading chamber by turning the handle clockwise. Place the material inside, ensuring it is properly aligned with the guide markers. Close the chamber securely.

Step 3: Start Processing
Press the START button. The machine will automatically process the material. The process typically takes 5-10 minutes depending on the material type.

Safety Features:

- Emergency Stop: Red button on the front panel
- Overload Protection: Automatically shuts down if overheated
- Safety Lock: Prevents operation when chamber is open

Maintenance:

Clean the machine weekly with a soft cloth. Do not use water or harsh chemicals. Lubricate moving parts monthly with provided lubricant.

Troubleshooting:

Problem: Machine won't start
Solution: Check power connection and ensure chamber is properly closed

Problem: Unusual noise during operation
Solution: Stop the machine immediately and check for obstructions
